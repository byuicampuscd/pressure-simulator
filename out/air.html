air

This module takes care of the air simulation, with the container, balls, and handle. Once included, it must be setup() before it will do anything. setup(settings) - Creates the balls representing the air setBallSpeed(newSpeed) - Sets the balls' speed to newSpeed px/s (within svg viewBox) startAnimation() - Makes the balls move; if none exist, it creates 50 first endAnimation() - Makes the balls stop
